★ On-Point ★

Welcome to the simple, yet amazing game of On Point where you can win points and game by dragging a slider.

Your goal is to place the slider as close as possible to the given target value. The closer you are, the more points you score. Enjoy!

If the slider matches exactly, you are given another additional 100 points.

If the slider is off by only 1, you are given another additional 50 points.
